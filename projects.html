My Thoughts on Learning...

The projects that I involve myself with are seemingly always technology-driven and centered around my main interests (aerospace, agriculture, art, business, design, and music). I learn by doing and am results driven. Therefore, I strive to learn subjects in a manner that will allow me to produce real-world results while I work toward my ultimate goal of creating a product or a business venture.

Learning through application presents the opportunity to do just that, as project-based learning forces one to think critically about a subject from multiple angles and use all available resources to come up with solutions to problems unique to the goal.

I have written a detailed report (.pdf) for each project that can be downloaded by clicking on the name of the project. All project source code (and resources) can also be found on my personal GitHub.
